Emergency Instructions :-

1.)Stay on line with 911 and follow emergency instructions.
2.)Stay calm and try to keep the patient calm.
3.)Don't move a patient who was injured in an automobile accident or fall,or who was found unconsious.
4.)If the patient cold,cover them with a blanket.
5.)Don't give an injured person anything to eat or drink(unless instructed by the 911 dispatcher).
6.)Have someone watch for the ambulance and show the crew how to get the patient.
